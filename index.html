Brand
Guidelines

This guide defines the visual language, design style, and principles that shape a clear and consistent brand experience, no matter the team or area of expertise.

This guide lays out the essential design standards that bring our brand to life and provides downloadable assets, from our color system and typography to accessibility benchmarks and documentation.

Whether you're designing for digital platforms or printed materials, these guidelines ensure every touchpoint reflects the trust and efficiency at the heart of Pixellot.

Logo Sizing

The logo minimum size for legibility is 75px wide, however the preferred size is 150-100px. When we need to go smaller or we have space constriction, use the O monogram instead.

GUIDELINES

Typography usage

Use Bold for title

Main headings

  • Red Hat Display Bold
  • Letter Spacing set to 0%
  • Line height should be 100-110%
  • Red Hat Display Bold Italic to highlight some words

Italic to highlight key words

Main headings

  • Red Hat Display Regular
  • Letter Spacing set to 0%
  • Line height should be 110%

Regular weight for subheadings and secondary titles.

Sub headings

  • Red Hat Display Regular
  • Letter Spacing set to 0%
  • Line height should be 110%

Body copy should be set in Red Hat Display Regular at a comfortable reading size. Keep paragraphs short and scannable for digital layouts.

Maintain generous line height and spacing between paragraphs to ensure readability across all screen sizes and devices.

Body copy

  • Red Hat Display Regular
  • Letter space should be set to 0%
  • Line Height should be 125%

Photography

Aim for authentic, real-world moments, so avoid heavy blurred or artificial backgrounds, overly polished shots, or "perfect" staged scenes. Capture true action, emotion, and interaction across practices, games, and sidelines. Include a natural mix of youth and genders to reflect the full range of players using Pixellot, and keep the style consistent with natural light, minimal editing, and clear context.
